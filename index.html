Digital ID – an Identity Layer for the Internet

Digital ID is an identity layer for the internet and a domain name system for personal information. It allows you to prove your identity towards other users and to look up their attributes in a decentralized manner. The ultimate goal of Digital ID is to replace closed platforms with open standards – one service at a time.
